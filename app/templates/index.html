About Quishy

Our Mission

At Quishy, we believe that convenience shouldn't require a compromise on security. As QR codes increasingly become the gateway to payments, menus, and digital services, they've also become a prime vector for cyberattacks. Our mission is to build an intelligent, frictionless shield that empowers you to scan the physical world without the fear of being phished, tracked, or scammed.

The Threat Landscape

The rise of "quishing" (QR phishing) has introduced invisible threats to everyday life. Attackers place malicious sticker overlays on legitimate parking meters or café tables, redirecting unaware victims to credential-harvesting websites or authorizing fraudulent UPI payments. Because the human eye cannot decipher a QR code's destination directly, these attacks have devastating success rates.

How It Works

  • Computer Vision Analysis: Uses trained ML models to detect physical anomalies like tampered or overlaid sticker artifacts on the QR code.
  • Link Verification: Employs Levenshtein distance matching alongside AI classification to block misspelled domains and verified threats.
  • Smart Payment Defense: Intercepts GPay and PhonePe QR codes natively, performing deep heuristic checks on the payee ID to prevent social engineering scams.
