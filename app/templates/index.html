About Us

Our Mission

At Quishy, we believe that curiosity shouldn't lead to a compromise. In an era where a single QR code or a shortened link can lead to identity theft, financial loss, or malware, we've hbuilt a shield. Our mission is simple: To empower users to explore the digital world without the fear of being phished.

The Problem
We're Solving

The "Quick Response" (QR) code was designed for convenience, but it has become a favorite tool for cybercriminals. From "quishing" (QR phishing) to malicious URL redirects, it is becoming harder to know what is on the other side of a scan.

Most people don't realize a link is dangerous until it's too late.
Quishy changes that.
